Why F1 is a Technical Marvel

Formula 1 is not just about speed; it's a showcase of cutting-edge engineering. The cars are masterpieces of aerodynamics, designed to generate immense downforce.

Drivers have access to systems like the DRS (Drag Reduction System) to aid in overtaking.

On some steering wheels, drivers press a button like Overtake to get a temporary power boost.
