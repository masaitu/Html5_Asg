What is DRS (Drag Reduction System)?

DRS is an adjustable rear wing on a Formula 1 car that the driver can open to reduce aerodynamic drag, increasing top speed to promote overtaking. It can only be used in specific "DRS zones" on the track when a car is within one second of the car ahead.
